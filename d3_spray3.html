New Blend modes for <feBlend>:

  • overlay

  • color-dodge

  • color-burn

  • hard-light

  • soft-light

  • difference

  • exclusion

  • hue

  • saturation

  • color

  • luminosity

Work in previous versions:

  • normal

  • multiply

  • screen

  • darken

  • lighten
